About Be My Muse!

Be The Real You!

The “Be My Muse” art organisation was established on the 1st of May 2020 (01/05/2020)
A.K.A during the start of the pandemic of Covid-19.

The organization was recognized to assist the people who were disadvantaged for them to find
work during the lockdown period, for them to create jobs for themselves and others.

After three years enough money was raised to build the establishment.
Different type of artists came to and began to contribute their artist ideas to sell and show case the art departments.

With enough money raised we were able to widespread our establishment to different parts of the
country as to create employment and to make things extra special there are now art schools available in all parts of the country.

The mission of the establishment is to prove that art can attract the attention of any individual, it could also be a source of income and allows the individual to express themselves and showcase their creativity.

The vision of the establishment is to be able to show the world that art is a very important aspect in life. Art can be expressed in various ways such as drawing, sketching, painting, dancing, singing, rapping, poetry, and writing.

The target audience for “Be My Muse!” is intended to individuals who value self-expression and creativity in the arts, suited for both aspiring and established artists.
