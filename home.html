Welcome To "Be My Muse!"

Welcome to "Be My Muse!" the art studio where we sell the finest books and poetry books.
Not only does the store sell books it sells art pieces that are both painted and drawn too.

The "Be My Muse!" values it's priorities by selling the best selling books that have been tailor made by the authors.
The books are found in different genres and as an addition we also have songbooks and poetry books too.

The store also offers lessons in singing, dancing, art, writing and many more ways to encourage people to express themselves in various ways in the art industry.

We offer painting, drawing and mixed-media courses for beginners through advanced students.

Weekly workshops focusing on poetry, short stories and creative non-fiction led by published authors.

Vocal coaching, instrumental lessons and dance classes for all ages and skill levels.
